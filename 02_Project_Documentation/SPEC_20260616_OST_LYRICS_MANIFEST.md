- **Role**: True Ending Theme (流浪地球 / 星际征服胜利)

*(Slow emotional piano, deep ambient synth, distant space resonance)*  
*(no vocals)*

A dying pulse beneath a dying sun.

## 🎵 2. 《Fate Beyond the Light Cone》 (光锥之外的命运 / 光锥之内的往事)
- **Role**: Platinum Achievement Theme (全图鉴白金结局 - 播放于最终 Credits)
- **Style/Prompt**: Epic cinematic space opera, emotional female lead vocal, Hans Zimmer style orchestral soundtrack, melancholic yet triumphant, slow piano intro, soaring strings, massive brass, deep percussion, emotional choir, cosmic loneliness, interstellar civilization, nostalgic and powerful, gradual build-up from intimate memories to universe-scale climax, scientific wonder, tragic beauty, high production value, emotional storytelling, female vocal with clear pronunciation, cinematic masterpiece, 78 BPM.

### Lyrics & Musical Script

#### [Intro]
*(Minimalist lonely piano, soft ambient strings, distant space ambience, slow emotional build-up, no vocals for first 20 seconds)*

#### [Verse 1]
*(Female Lead, soulful, nostalgic and quiet)*  
We mapped the orbits in the golden haze,  
And watched the pendulum of dying days.  
Through cold deterrence and the broadcast cries,  
We etched our names across the falling skies.  
We crossed the silence of the endless night,  
Still carrying fragments of a fading light.  
Though stars grew distant and the years were gone,  
We kept the memory of where we came from.  

#### [Pre-Chorus]
*(Strings swell, subtle ticking percussion enters)*  
A silent forest where the hunters tread,  
We hid our heartbeats in the darkest bed.  
But logic failed and the dimensions tore,  
Leaving the ashes on a masterless shore.  
The signal faded into cosmic foam,  
Yet every wandering soul still searched for home.  

#### [Chorus]
*(Explosive Hans Zimmer style climax, full orchestra, powerful emotional female vocals, soaring strings and brass)*  
Oh, all the monuments we built to soar,  
Are fading echoes of a cosmic war.  
Outside the light cone where the physics bend,  
We are the stories at the universe's end.  
A fleeting spark among the cosmic dust,  
A dream preserved beyond decay and rust.  
Though stars grow silent and the ages roam,  
The light we carried still will lead us home.  

#### [Interlude]
*(Choir and orchestra briefly pull back, ethereal female vocalise)*  
Ahhh——  

#### [Verse 2]
*(Piano returns beneath orchestral strings)*  
The engines slept beneath a frozen sky,  
While countless generations passed us by.  
We built our hopes from mathematics and steel,  
And learned how fragile every truth could feel.  
The galaxies unfolded one by one,  
Yet every journey ended where begun.  
For every empire scattered into stone,  
A single memory remained unknown.  

#### [Pre-Chorus]
*(More emotional than first pre-chorus)*  
The clocks ran down, the final stars grew dim,  
Yet still we sang the ancient pilgrim hymn.  
Beyond all victory, beyond all blame,  
Only remembrance kept the fading flame.  

#### [Final Chorus]
*(Maximum emotional climax, massive choir joins orchestra)*  
Oh, all the monuments we built to soar,  
Are fading echoes of a cosmic war.  
Outside the light cone where the physics bend,  
We are the stories at the universe's end.  
A fleeting spark among the cosmic dust,  
A dream preserved beyond decay and rust.  
Though all things vanish into night unknown,  
We leave our footprints written in the stars alone.  

#### [Outro]
*(Music suddenly drops out, fading back to solitary piano and ambient space sounds)*  
The epochs fade, the sandbox now is still...  
A dust of memories time cannot kill...  
*(Soft female whisper)*  
We remember.  
*(Spoken word, distant and calm)*  
We were here.
