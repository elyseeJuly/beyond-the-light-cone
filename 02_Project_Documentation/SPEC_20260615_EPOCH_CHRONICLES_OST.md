## **🎙️ 第二部：结局与终章 (Endings & Epilogues)**

### **1\. 真结局主题曲：《Stardust Exodus》（星屑） \[含人声\]**

* **触发条件：** 流浪地球启航 / 星际征服胜利  
* **曲风提示词：** J-RPG space opera soundtrack, symphonic EDM mixed with orchestral rock, high energy, ethereal but powerful anime-style female vocals, dramatic choir, grand cosmic epic.  
* *(歌词采用已定稿的《Stardust Exodus》专属歌词)*

### **2\. 概念结局：黑域 / 数字永生 \[纯器乐 Instrumental\]**

* **《Death of the Light Cone》（光锥之死）- 黑域结局：** \> \[Instrumental\] Slow requiem, dark ambient classical, wordless sacred choir, tragic and solemn, trapped in eternity, fading tempo.  
* **《Ghost in the Quantum》（量子幽灵）- 数字结局：** \> \[Instrumental\] Cyberpunk synthwave, digital glitch effects, mechanical beats, emotionless data stream vibe, cold ambient synthesizer, no human vocals.

### **3\. 隐藏结局：《The Last Archive》（最后的档案） \[纯器乐 Instrumental\]**

* **触发条件：** 文明自然衰亡。  
* **AI 提示词：** \> \[Instrumental\] Melancholic solo piano, tape hiss, old radio static, fading memories, gentle oblivion, time passing by, ending with a distinct page turning sound, absolute silence.

### **4\. Fate Beyond the Light Cone：《A Past Within the Light Cone》（光锥之内的往事） \[含人声\]**

* **触发条件：** 解锁所有胜利与失败结局，于最终制作人员名单（Credits）播放。  
* **曲风提示词：** \> Cinematic emotional masterpiece, starts with minimalist lonely piano, slowly building into full cinematic orchestral climax. Soulful and powerful female vocal, nostalgic, overwhelmingly emotional, Hans Zimmer style epic finish.
